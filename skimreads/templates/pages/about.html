One Sentence

Skim online content by reading user-generated notes.

What is Skimreads

Have you ever finished reading something and told yourself "I could have wrote that entire thing in a couple sentences?"

Online content often contains too many fillers, just like Dragon Ball Z. Sometimes there are only a few parts that you can relate to and the rest is just fluff.

We are the annotations* of the web. There is just way too much stuff on the internet to read all by yourself. Together we can cut the bologna, skim the fat, and read lean.

*An annotation is a note that is made while reading any form of text. This may be as simple as underlining or highlighting passages. (courtesy of Wikipedia)

How it Works

  1. Skim user readings by looking at their notes
  2. Add notes of your own to other people's reading
  3. Save your online content, make notes, and let others skim
